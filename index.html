Our Story

Born from a love of plants, flavour, and community — welcome to Vegan Garden.

Who we are

We're a small, independent vegan food stall based in the heart of Greenwich Market. Every item on our menu is made from scratch — baked fresh each morning and cooked with care. No shortcuts, no preservatives, just real food.

What drives us

We believe that vegan food doesn't have to be boring. Our menu blends hearty savoury dishes — from rich vegan lasagna to stuffed roasted vegetables — with homemade cakes and pastries that taste like they came from someone's kitchen. Because they did.
